In-Clinic Emergency Protocol

All clinical staff at Seetha Dental Lounge are trained in Basic Life Support (BLS) and dental emergency management. Our clinic is equipped with an emergency kit including oxygen, epinephrine, and AED access.

  1. 1 Stop treatment immediately and assess the patient.
  2. 2 Alert the senior dentist and clinic staff.
  3. 3 Administer first aid and retrieve the emergency kit.
  4. 4 Call 108 if hospital transfer is required.
  5. 5 Notify the patient's emergency contact.
medication

Allergic Reactions

Always inform us of any known allergies before treatment. In case of anaphylaxis, epinephrine is available on-site and 108 will be called immediately.

favorite

Cardiac Events

Staff are trained in CPR. In the event of cardiac arrest, CPR is initiated immediately while 108 is called. AED access is available nearby.

air

Fainting / Syncope

Patient is placed in a supine position with legs elevated. Oxygen is administered if needed. Vital signs are monitored until recovery.

bloodtype

Excessive Bleeding

Direct pressure is applied immediately. Haemostatic agents are available. If bleeding is uncontrolled, the patient is referred to a hospital.

dental

Dental Emergencies After Hours

For dental emergencies outside clinic hours — such as severe toothache, knocked-out tooth, or dental abscess — call our clinic line at 080753 33723. For life-threatening emergencies, call 108 immediately.

💡 Knocked-out tooth tip: Keep the tooth moist in milk or saliva and get to a dentist within 30 minutes for the best chance of re-implantation.

Nearest Hospital

The nearest government hospital is Paravur Taluk Hospital, located approximately 1 km from our clinic. For serious emergencies, call 108 for ambulance dispatch.
