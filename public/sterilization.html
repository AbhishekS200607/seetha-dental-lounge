Our Sterilization Standard

At Seetha Dental Lounge, patient safety is our highest priority. Every instrument used in treatment undergoes a strict multi-step sterilization process compliant with Indian Dental Association (IDA) and WHO guidelines.

cleaning_services

Pre-Cleaning

All instruments are pre-soaked in enzymatic solution immediately after use to break down organic matter before mechanical cleaning.

water

Ultrasonic Cleaning

Instruments are processed in an ultrasonic cleaner to remove all debris from hard-to-reach areas before sterilization.

local_fire_department

Autoclave Sterilization

All reusable instruments are sterilized in a Class B autoclave at 134°C for a minimum of 18 minutes, killing all bacteria, viruses, and spores.

inventory_2

Sealed Packaging

Sterilized instruments are sealed in pouches with chemical indicators and stored in a clean, dry environment until use.

masks

Single-Use Disposables

Needles, gloves, suction tips, and saliva ejectors are single-use only and disposed of safely after each patient.

chair

Surface Disinfection

Dental chairs, light handles, and all contact surfaces are disinfected with hospital-grade disinfectant between every patient.

fact_check

Compliance & Audits

Our sterilization procedures are reviewed and audited regularly. Autoclave performance is tested with biological indicators weekly. All staff are trained in infection control protocols annually. We maintain a physical sterilization log at the clinic available for inspection upon request.
