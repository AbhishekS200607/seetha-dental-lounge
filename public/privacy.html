Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when registering or booking an appointment — including your full name, phone number, and email address. We also collect appointment and token data to manage your queue position and visit history.

database

How We Use Your Data

  • • To manage your appointment tokens and queue position
  • • To contact you regarding your appointment status
  • • To improve our clinic services and patient experience
  • • To maintain accurate medical and visit records
lock

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Only authorised clinic staff can access patient records.

share

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to your treating doctor and authorised clinic administrators. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
